About Us

Mission: We aim to expand mental healthcare access on college campuses through video chat therapy with licensed mental health professionals.

By college students, for college students.

Over 40% of college students have anxiety and another 36% are coping with depression -- and on average, there is 1 campus mental health worker for every 1000 students. Counseling centers are swamped, but waitlists are not the only issue. Some students don’t seek help because they wonder if their situation even warrants help.
  With the need for mental health services on college campuses rising, we want to reduce barriers to seeking care that currently exist. After speaking with over thirty college counseling directors from across the country and interviewing dozens of students, we came to video chat therapy as a viable, effective way to increase access to mental health services on campus.
